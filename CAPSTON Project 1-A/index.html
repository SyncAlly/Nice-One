Understanding Pollution

Pollution is the introduction of harmful materials into the environment. These harmful materials are called pollutants and can be natural or created by human activity. Pollutants damage the quality of air, water, and land.

This website explores various types of pollution affecting our planet and provides actionable solutions for a cleaner future.
